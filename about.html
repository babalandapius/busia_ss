Our Foundation

Mission, Vision & Core Values

🎯

Our Mission

To provide quality, holistic secondary education that equips students with the knowledge, skills, values, and competencies needed for lifelong learning, productive citizenship, and meaningful contribution to Uganda and the global community.

🌟

Our Vision

To be the leading secondary school in Uganda, recognized for academic excellence, moral integrity, innovation, and the holistic development of every student into a responsible, competent, and visionary leader.

Our Heritage

46 Years of Academic Legacy

Founded in 1978 by the Busia District Education Committee with just 4 classrooms and 120 students, Busia Secondary School has grown into one of Eastern Uganda's most prestigious institutions, now serving nearly 3,000 students from across the country.

Through decades of dedicated leadership, community support, and a commitment to excellence, the school has produced thousands of graduates who now serve Uganda in medicine, law, engineering, business, politics, and the arts.

Our growth from a modest district school to a nationally recognized centre of excellence is a testament to the hard work of our staff, the dedication of our students, and the unwavering support of the Busia community.

Frequently Asked Questions

Busia Secondary School was founded in 1978 by the Busia District Education Committee. We have been providing quality education for over 46 years and have grown to become one of the leading secondary schools in Eastern Uganda.
Busia Secondary School is a government-aided school, which means it receives partial funding from the Uganda government under the Universal Secondary Education (USE) program, while also charging supplementary fees to support quality education and facilities maintenance.
Yes. We have modern boarding facilities for both boys and girls, accommodating up to 1,200 students. Our hostels have 24-hour security, clean ablution blocks, and comfortable dormitories. Day scholars are also welcome.
