Our Programmes

Uganda Certificate of
Education (UCE)

Our O-Level programme (Senior 1 to Senior 4) follows the NCDC curriculum and prepares students for the Uganda Certificate of Education examinations. We offer a broad and balanced curriculum covering sciences, humanities, languages, and technical subjects.

Students sit for a minimum of 8 subjects and a maximum of 10 subjects at UCE. Our 2024 results saw 98% pass rate with 15 students attaining Division 1 and 47 students Division 2.

Uganda Advanced
Certificate of Education

Our A-Level programme (Senior 5 and Senior 6) prepares students for the Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education and direct university entry. Students choose from a range of subject combinations and are guided by specialist teachers with deep subject knowledge.

In 2024, 94% of our A-Level students qualified for direct university admission, with 12 students earning government sponsorships to Makerere University.
