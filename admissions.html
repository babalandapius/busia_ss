Admissions 2025/2026

Join Uganda's leading secondary school. Applications are open for S.1 and S.5 intake. Learn about requirements, fees, and how to apply.

Entry Criteria

Admission Requirements

🎓

Senior One (S.1) Entry

  • ✅ PLE aggregate of 20 or better
  • ✅ Original PLE certificate / results slip
  • ✅ Baptism / Birth certificate (copy)
  • ✅ 4 recent passport photos
  • ✅ Completed application form
  • ✅ Application fee: UGX 20,000
  • ✅ Medical fitness report
📖

Senior Five (S.5) Entry

  • ✅ UCE aggregate 32 or better
  • ✅ Credits in desired A-Level combination
  • ✅ Original UCE certificate / results slip
  • ✅ Transfer letter (for transfers)
  • ✅ 4 recent passport photos
  • ✅ Application fee: UGX 25,000
  • ✅ Completed application form
📋

Application Process

  • 1️⃣ Fill inquiry form below
  • 2️⃣ Collect application form at office
  • 3️⃣ Attach required documents
  • 4️⃣ Pay application fee
  • 5️⃣ Submit to admissions office
  • 6️⃣ Await selection letter
  • 7️⃣ Report with fees balance
