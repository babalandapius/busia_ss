Our Location

Busia Secondary School is located in Busia Town, Eastern Uganda, easily accessible by road from Kampala (270 km), Jinja (150 km), and the Kenya border (2 km).

Busia Secondary School

By Bus
Direct buses from Kampala (Post Bus, Link Bus) to Busia. 4-5 hrs.

By Road
From Kampala via Jinja on the Tororo–Busia Highway. 4 hrs drive.

Boda Boda
From Busia Town centre, ask for "BSS" or "Busia Secondary School". 5 minutes.
