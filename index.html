Headteacher's Welcome

A Message from Our Headteacher

Welcome to Busia Secondary School — a place where every student's potential is discovered, nurtured, and celebrated. Since our founding in 1978, we have maintained an unwavering commitment to academic excellence, moral integrity, and the overall development of young people.

Our dedicated faculty, modern facilities, and vibrant school culture create an environment where students don't just learn — they thrive. We prepare young men and women to face the challenges of a rapidly changing world with confidence, compassion, and competence.

We are proud of our consistently high UNEB results, our nationally recognized sports programs, and the leadership roles our alumni hold across Uganda and beyond. I invite you to join the Busia Secondary School family.

Learn More About Us →
| Mr. Aggrey Mayende, Headteacher
Why Choose Us

A School Built on Excellence

We combine rigorous academics with holistic development to produce well-rounded graduates ready for university and life.

Academic Excellence

98% UNEB pass rate with multiple Division 1 students each year. Strong O-Level and A-Level programs.

Modern Laboratories

Fully equipped Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Computer laboratories supporting hands-on learning.

Sports Excellence

Regional champions in football, athletics, and netball. Professional coaching and modern sports facilities.

Arts & Culture

Vibrant music, drama, and debate clubs that develop creativity, communication, and confidence.

Strong Values

Character education and pastoral care ensuring every student develops strong moral values and discipline.

Digital Learning

Computer lab with internet access, digital classrooms, and ICT integration across all subjects.
