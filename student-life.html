Student Life

At Busia Secondary School, education goes far beyond the classroom. Discover the vibrant clubs, sports, leadership opportunities, and community that make student life truly extraordinary.

Extra-Curricular

Clubs & Societies

With over 32 clubs and societies, every student finds their passion and builds skills beyond the classroom.

The Drama Club produces two major stage productions per year and participates in the National Drama Festival. Members develop acting, directing, scriptwriting, and stagecraft skills. The club has won the Eastern Uganda Drama Festival trophy 3 years running. Open to all students; no experience required — just passion and commitment.

Meeting day: Wednesdays, 2:30 PM | Teacher patron: Mr. Lwanga | Members: 65
Our award-winning choir and music club encompasses traditional Ugandan music, contemporary gospel, and classical styles. Members perform at school functions, regional music festivals, and community events. The club has produced several musicians who have gone on to national recognition.

Meeting day: Tuesdays & Thursdays, 4:00 PM | Teacher patron: Mrs. Akello | Members: 80
The Debate Club trains students in structured argumentation, critical thinking, and public speaking. Members compete in interschool, regional, and national debate competitions. In 2025, the club won the Eastern Uganda Regional Debate Championship. The club also coaches students for Model UN participation.

Meeting day: Fridays, 3:00 PM | Teacher patron: Mrs. Nalwanga | Members: 45
Students conduct research projects, build working models, and participate in the National Science Fair. Recent projects include a solar-powered water pump for the school garden and a low-cost water purification system. The club partners with Makerere University engineering students for mentorship.

Meeting day: Wednesdays, 3:30 PM | Teacher patron: Dr. Achieng | Members: 55
Champions environmental conservation through tree planting, waste management, and climate change awareness campaigns. Members planted 500 trees in Busia Town in 2025 and run the school's composting and recycling programme. Partners with the National Forestry Authority and NEMA Uganda.

Meeting day: Saturdays, 8:00 AM | Teacher patron: Mr. Egesa | Members: 90
Members learn web development, Python programming, and robotics using Arduino. The club participates in the Uganda National Robotics Competition and has won medals at the East African ICT Summit. Several graduates have gone on to study Computer Science and Software Engineering.

Meeting day: Tuesdays, 3:00 PM | Teacher patron: Mr. Opio | Members: 40
Produces the termly school magazine "The Busia Beacon", runs the school's social media accounts, and trains students in print and digital journalism, photography, and videography. Members attend the Uganda Schools Media Festival annually.

Meeting day: Fridays, 2:30 PM | Teacher patron: Mr. Mugisha | Members: 35
Organises community outreach programmes including free tutoring for primary school pupils, hospital visits, elderly care, and fundraising for vulnerable families. The club models Ubuntu values — "I am because we are." Awarded Best Community Club by Busia District Council in 2024.

Meeting day: Saturdays, 9:00 AM | Teacher patron: Mrs. Atim | Members: 120
Athletics & Games

Sports Programme

Sports are a cornerstone of life at Busia SS. We believe in healthy minds, healthy bodies, and the team spirit that sport builds.

⚽ Football (Soccer)
Our Lions football team is one of the most decorated in Eastern Uganda, training 5 days a week under professional coaches.
🏆 Eastern Uganda Champions 2025
🏐 Netball
Our girls netball team has been regional champions for 4 consecutive years and placed 3rd at the National Schools Championship.
🥈 National Schools 2024
🏃 Athletics & Track
Our athletics team competes in sprints, long-distance, hurdles, field events, and cross-country at regional and national levels.
⭐ 5 National Medalists 2024
🏀 Basketball
Both boys and girls teams participate in the USSSA Basketball competitions, developing skills in ball handling, teamwork, and strategy.
🎯 Regional Finalists 2024
🏐 Volleyball
Volleyball is popular with both boarding and day students. Teams compete in the USSSA volleyball league with inter-house tournaments each term.
🟡 Active Programme
🏓 Table Tennis & Chess
Indoor sports programmes developing concentration, reflexes, and strategic thinking. Open to all students as part of the school's games period.
🏅 District Champions 2024
Arts & Culture

Debate, Music &
Performing Arts

Busia SS has a distinguished tradition of excellence in the arts. Our debate teams regularly win regional and national competitions, and our music and drama groups have performed at the national level.

  • 🗣️ Annual Inter-Class Debate Competition — every student participates
  • 🎵 Music & Dance Festival — traditional and contemporary categories
  • 🎭 Annual Drama Production — full-cast play each term
  • 🎤 School Choir — performs at national functions and churches
  • 📝 Creative Writing Competition — fiction, poetry, essays
  • 🎨 Art Exhibition — held annually at the end of Term 3

The Student Council

The Student Council comprises 24 elected students who represent their peers on matters of school policy, welfare, academics, and activities. Elections are held at the beginning of each academic year and promote democratic values and civic responsibility.

Residential Life

Boarding Experience

For boarding students, life at Busia SS is a rich, structured, and supportive community experience. Our dormitories are home to over 1,200 students who build lasting friendships and develop independence.

  • 🛏️ Clean, comfortable dormitories with 24-hour security
  • 🍽️ Three nutritious meals daily in the school dining hall
  • 📚 Supervised evening preps 7:00 PM – 9:30 PM daily
  • 🏥 On-site medical clinic and visiting doctor
  • ⚡ Solar-powered backup electricity for studying
  • 📱 Structured phone access on weekends for family contact
  • 🧹 Housemaster/mistress support and pastoral care
